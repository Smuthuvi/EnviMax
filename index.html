Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our environmental health and public health consultancy services, processes, and solutions.

1. What services does EnviMax offer?

We provide environmental health consultancy, public health research, health education and promotion, training and capacity building, WASH services and monitoring and evaluation. Our solutions are evidence-based and tailored to meet client needs.

2. Who can benefit from your services?

Our services are designed for individuals, communities, institutions, NGOs, schools, healthcare facilities, and government organizations seeking professional public and environmental health solutions.

3. Do you handle both small and large projects?

Yes. We handle projects of all sizes—from small community interventions to large institutional and government consultancy assignments—offering scalable and flexible solutions.

4. How do I request your services?

You can contact us through our website contact form, email, or phone. We will review your needs and provide a customized proposal or consultation plan.

5. Are your services based on scientific evidence?

Absolutely. All our interventions are grounded in scientific research, public health standards and best environmental health practices to ensure effective and sustainable outcomes.
